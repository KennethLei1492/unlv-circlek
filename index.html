Who We Are

Growing Together in Service

Welcome to UNLV Circle K International — a student-led service organization dedicated to making a difference on campus and in our greater Las Vegas community. We're part of the Moonlight Division and proud to be one of the most active CKI chapters in the district.

Whether you're passionate about volunteering, developing leadership skills, or just want to meet amazing people — Circle K is your community. We hope to see you at a future general meeting!!! ^_^

Learn How to Join

🌿 Service

Giving back to our campus and community through hands-on volunteering and outreach initiatives throughout the year.

✨ Leadership

Developing the skills and confidence to lead both inside and outside of Circle K — shaping tomorrow's change-makers.

🤝 Fellowship

Building lifelong friendships and a supportive family of like-minded peers who share a passion for making a difference.
